❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the tour suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or a first-time explorer, our guides tailor the experience to all fitness and comfort levels.

What should I pack?

We recommend warm layers, hiking boots, a waterproof jacket, and a good sense of adventure. We'll provide all camping and activity gear.

Is the tour weather dependent?

Rain or shine, the Vikings pressed on — and so will we! However, some activities may be adjusted in case of extreme weather.
